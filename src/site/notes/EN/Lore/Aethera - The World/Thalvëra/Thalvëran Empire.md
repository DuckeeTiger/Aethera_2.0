Compared to the age of the world itself, the Thalvëran Empire is still very young, only a few hundred years old. Throughout history, there were many attempts to unite the species and kingdoms of the continent of Thalvëra under a single empire, but every attempt eventually collapsed.

Around 300 years ago, most kingdoms were struggling with civil wars and slowly consuming themselves from within. This era created a true opportunity for someone to seize power and unite the fractured kingdoms under one banner.

The ruler of [[EN/Lore/Aethera - The World/Thalvëra/Kingdom of Eryndor\|Kingdom of Eryndor]] started a conquest to "unite" the continent and it was successful. With the lead of [[EN/Lore/Aethera - The World/Thalvëra/Kingdom of Eryndor\|Eryndor]] the Thalvëran Empire was established and the following Kingdoms "joined": [[EN/Lore/Aethera - The World/Thalvëra/Kingdom of Thalanor\|Kingdom of Thalanor]], [[EN/Lore/Aethera - The World/Thalvëra/Kingdom of Kharindor\|Kingdom of Kharindor]], [[EN/Lore/Aethera - The World/Thalvëra/Aegeryn Sea League\|Aegeryn Sea League]]

There were attempts to add the two southern Kingdoms to the Empire - [[EN/Lore/Aethera - The World/Thalvëra/Kingdom of Myramar\|Kingdom of Myramar]] and [[EN/Lore/Aethera - The World/Thalvëra/Kingdom of Aldemar\|Kingdom of Aldemar]] but these attempts were often unsuccessful. The land - [[Ryvia\|Ryvia]] - where the two kingdoms are located is difficult to access from the north due to the mountains that border the region, so it is difficult to reach by land.
