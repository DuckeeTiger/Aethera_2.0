> # Arcane Vigor
>
> *2nd level Abjuration ([[EN/Player Options/Classes/Sorcerer/Sorcerer\|Sorcerer]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Wizard/Wizard\|Wizard]])*  
> **Casting Time:** Bonus  
> **Range:** Self  
> **Components:** V, S  
> **Duration:** Instantaneous
>
> You tap into life force to heal yourself. Roll one or two of your unexpended Hit Point Dice, and regain a number of Hit Points equal to the roll's total plus your spellcasting ability modifier. Those dice are then expended.
>
> **Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The number of unexpended Hit Dice you can roll increases by one for each spell slot level above 2.
