> # Armor of Agathys
>
> *1st level Abjuration ([[Artificer\|Artificer]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Warlock/Warlock\|Warlock]])*  
> **Casting Time:** Bonus Action
> **Range:** Self  
> **Components:** V, S, M (a shard of blue glass)  
> **Duration:** 1 hour
>
> Protective magical frost surrounds you. You gain 5 Temporary Hit Points. If a creature hits you with a melee attack roll before the spell ends, the creature takes 5 Cold damage. The spell ends early if you have no Temporary Hit Points.
>
> **Using a Higher-Level Spell Slots:** The Temporary Hit Points and the Cold damage both increase by 5 for each spell slots level above 1.
