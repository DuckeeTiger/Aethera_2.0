> # Aura of Life
>
> *4th level Abjuration ([[Artificer\|Artificer]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Cleric/Cleric\|Cleric]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Paladin/Paladin\|Paladin]])*  
> **Casting Time:** Action  
> **Range:** Self (30-foot emanation)  
> **Components:** V  
> **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes
>
> An aura radiates from you in a 30-foot Emanation for the duration. While in the aura, you and your allies have Resistance to Necrotic damage, and your Hit Point maximums can't be reduced.
>
> If an ally with 0 Hit Points starts its turn in the aura, that ally regains 1 Hit Point.
