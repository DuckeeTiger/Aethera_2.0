> # Beast Sense
>
> *2nd level Divination (ritual) ([[Artificer\|Artificer]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Druid/Druid\|Druid]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Ranger/Ranger\|Ranger]])*
> **Casting Time:** Action
> **Range:** Touch
> **Components:** S
> **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour
>
> You touch a willing Beast. For the duration, you can perceive through the Beast's senses, you benefit from any special senses it has.
