> [!spell|no-t]
> # Blinding Smite
>
> *3rd level Evocation ([[Artificer\|Artificer]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Paladin/Paladin\|Paladin]])*
> **Casting Time:** Bonus Action
> **Range:** Self
> **Components:** V
> **Duration:** 1 minute
>
> The target hit by the strike takes an extra 3d8 Radiant damage from the attack, and the target has the [[Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Blinded\|Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Blinded]] condition until the spell ends. At the end of each of its turns, the Blinded target makes a Constitution saving throw, ending the spell on itself on a success.
>
> **Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The extra damage increases by 1d8 for each spell slot level above 3.
