> # Crusader's Mantle
>
> *3rd level Evocation ([[EN/Player Options/Classes/Paladin/Paladin\|Paladin]])*
> **Casting Time:** Action
> **Range:** Self
> **Components:** V
> **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
