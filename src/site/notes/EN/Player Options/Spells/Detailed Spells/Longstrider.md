> # Longstrider
>
> _Level 1 Transmutation ([[Artificer\|Artificer]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Bard/Bard\|Bard]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Druid/Druid\|Druid]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Ranger/Ranger\|Ranger]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Wizard/Wizard\|Wizard]])_
> **Casting Time:** Action  
> **Range:** Touch  
> **Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of dirt)  
> **Duration:** 1 hour
>
> You touch a creature. The target’s Speed increases by 10 feet until the spell ends.
>
> ***Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.*** You can target one additional creature for each spell slot level above 1.
