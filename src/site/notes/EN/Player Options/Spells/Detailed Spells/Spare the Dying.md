> # Spare the Dying
>
> _Necromancy Cantrip ([[EN/Player Options/Classes/Cleric/Cleric\|Cleric]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Druid/Druid\|Druid]])_
> **Casting Time:** Action
> **Range:** 15 feet
> **Components:** V, S
> **Duration:** Instantaneous
> Choose a creature within range that has 0 Hit Points and isn’t dead. The creature becomes Stable.
>
> **_Cantrip Upgrade._** The range doubles when you reach levels 5 (30 feet), 11 (60 feet), and 17 (120 feet).
