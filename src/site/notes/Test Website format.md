## header 2
Timeline Test

> [!timeline|t-l] **The Birth of Racriaven** _Primordial Age_
> [[HU/Lore/Aethera - A világ/Thalvëra/Racriaven\|Racriaven]] is shaped by Racelion and Viraen, becoming the first whole world before the Shattering.

> [!timeline|t-r] **The Age of Creation** _Before the great wars_
> The gods, Elemental Sovereigns, and first mortal peoples shape the lands, seas, cultures, and early powers of the world.

> [!timeline|t-l] **The War of the Eidolons** _First great divine war_
> The Eidolons turn creation against itself, drawing gods, Sovereigns, mortals, and dragons into a conflict that breaks the old order.

> [!timeline|t-r] **The Shattering** _Birth of Aethera_
> Racriaven is broken, and the world becomes Aethera. The [[EN/Lore/Aethera - The World/Sundrakar/The Aether Sea\|Aether Sea]] forms as the wound where reality, divine power, and planar forces overlap.

> [!timeline|t-l] **The Age of Dragons** _After the gods fall silent_
> Dragons inherit the wounded world. Bahamut and Tiamat become the great opposing powers of dragonkind.

> [!timeline|t-r] **The War of Dragons** _End of draconic dominion_
> The Metallic and Chromatic flights tear the skies apart. The war ends with Tiamat’s defeat, Bahamut’s withdrawal, and the beginning of mortal rule.

> [!timeline|t-l] **Founding of the Thalveran Empire** _Early Age of Reclaiming_
> The [[Thalveran Empire\|Thalveran Empire]] is founded after the War of Dragons as a survival pact between the realms of [[Thalvëra\|Thalvëra]].

> [!timeline|t-r] **The Age of Imperial Unity** _First centuries of the Empire_
> Imperial roads, shared laws, fortified routes, and the High Imperial Council bind much of Thalvëra into a single political order.

> [!timeline|t-l] **The Long Unraveling** _Decline of imperial authority_
> The Empire loosens over centuries. [[EN/Lore/Aethera - The World/Thalvëra/Old Elven Domain\|The Old Elven Domain]] remains autonomous, while the [[EN/Lore/Aethera - The World/Thalvëra/Aegeryn Sea League\|Aegeryn Sea League]] becomes tied more by treaty than obedience.

> [!timeline|t-r] **The Fracture of Ryvia** _Southern secession_
> Ryvia drifts from imperial control and later divides into [[EN/Lore/Aethera - The World/Thalvëra/Kingdom of Myramar\|Myramar]] and [[Kingdom Aldemar\|Aldemar]].

> [!timeline|t-l] **The Empire in the Present Day** _A crown of memory_
> The Empire survives through [[EN/Lore/Aethera - The World/Thalvëra/Kingdom of Eryndor\|Eryndor]], [[EN/Lore/Aethera - The World/Thalvëra/Kingdom of Thalanor\|Thalanor]], and [[EN/Lore/Aethera - The World/Thalvëra/Kingdom of Kharindor\|Kharindor]], but exists more as law, symbol, and political theatre than true central power.
